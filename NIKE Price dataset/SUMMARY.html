Project Executive Summary

This pipeline processes historical stock dataset of Nike (NKE) ranging from year 2000 to 2026. The target metric is stock trading Volume, modeled using price dynamics (Open, High, Low, Close) and temporal factors (Year, Month, Day) trained through a multi-layer deep neural network.
